About

Jesse Levi Valentine, better known online as F-777, is a Canadian YouTuber and electronic musician, known for creating music varying orchestral music to techno and dance.

He was born on March 28, 1993, and starting making music during 2008, at the age of 14, after making animations for 5 years. He spends his time making freelance soundtracks for games, such as Geometry Dash and Rolling Sky where some of his songs were present on their official levels. He is one of the top artists on Newgrounds and was creating music as early as 2008 when he was posting to both YouTube and MySpace. Some early tracks include "Speedbreaker, "Before Mydnite," "Air Fantasy" and "Alone". He also created orchestration songs for the online game Dark Relic.

Jesse has been known for releasing a massive amount of albums over the years (usually two or three annually) , but were only released through his Bandcamp page. However, by 2020, he released all of his discography to all streaming platforms. Some of his popular albums include Raptor, Pirate Dance Machine, and Deadlocked.
