DJ-Nate

Introduction

Nathan Ingalls, known as DJ-Nate, is an American DJ and musical productor. He's a popular artist inside Geometry Dash, having 4 of his songs featured in the main levels and multiple more being used by the users in custom levels.

About

He was born on March 5th, 1991, in Chicago, Illinois. He started producing music on 2005. However, it wasn't until 2010 where he published his first hit: Electrodynamix,which eventually would find its way into Geometry Dash, being his third song to achieve that after Theory of Everything and Clubstep, probably his most iconic and known song.

On 2015, after an update to the game that allowed custom songs to be used on levels, his popularity exploded once more. Lots of players started using his songs on their own levels, some of which became icons of the game, like Ice Carbon Diablo X, The Golden, Acheron or Amethyst, all of which were the hardest GD level of their time.

He mostly stopped publishing music after 2018, with his last relevant song being Final Theory, the fourth and last part of his Theory of Everything series, released on March 9th, 2020.
