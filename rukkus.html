DJ-Nate

Introduction

Stephen Crouse, known in Newgrounds as Rukkus, was a musician specialized on the dubstep genre. He is most know for his song Nine Circles, which was featured in one of the most popular GD levels of all time, with most of his other songs being similarly used for remakes of the level.

About

He joined Newgrounds in 2007, initially making songs in the Techno, Dance, and Classical genres, before moving to Dubstep in 2010. He made his first attempt at Dubstep - wip - in November 2010. Later that year, he made his first Dubstep song, Nightfall His 2 main genres in 2011 were Techno and Dubstep. In that same year, he participated in the 2011 Newgrounds Audio Death Match.

In 2012, he began to move away from the Techno Genre and focused in the Dubstep and House genres, and in 2013, he made some very popular songs of his, including Nine Circles and Jawbreaker. He stopped making songs after 2014 due to graduating college and Pursuing a career in Accounting. Though he has stated multiple times his desire to continue making music, including as lately as this year, he still hasn't.
